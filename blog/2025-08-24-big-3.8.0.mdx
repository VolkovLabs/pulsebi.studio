# Business Intelligence 3.8.0: Elevating Data-Driven Decision Making

In today’s data-driven world, staying ahead requires tools that deliver innovation and efficiency. We’re thrilled to introduce **Business Intelligence 3.8.0**, a transformative update to the [Business Intelligence platform](/docs/) packed with advanced features, intuitive UI enhancements, and significant performance improvements.

This release empowers users with smarter, faster, and more secure ways to harness data for impactful decisions. Let’s explore the key innovations in Business Intelligence 3.8.0 and how they can revolutionize your workflows.

## What’s New in Business Intelligence 3.8.0?

Business Intelligence 3.8.0 brings a host of powerful updates, from streamlined alert and report management to performance optimizations and user-centric design improvements. With flexible scheduling, enhanced engine controls, and robust security, this release ensures a seamless experience for all users.

Here’s a quick overview of the highlights:

- **Engine Management:** Start and pause functionalities for effortless control during maintenance or downtime.
- **Alert Management:** Simplified configurations with a dedicated Event Configuration tab and relocated dashboard variable options.
- **Report Details:** New download image overlay for easy sharing and documentation, mirroring alert screenshots.
- **Reports UI:** Polished interface with additional pages and clear feedback, even when no data is available.
- **Timezone Scheduling:** Customizable timezones for alerts and reports, perfect for global teams.
- **Performance:** Optimized JSON viewer and UI components for faster load times and smoother interactions.
- **Auto Thresholds:** Enhanced algorithms for precise anomaly detection and adaptive alerting.
- **Actions:** Flexible start and pause options to manage notifications during maintenance or testing.

Let’s dive deeper into these features and see how they can elevate your experience with Grafana and beyond.

## Engine Management: Control at Your Fingertips

Managing data engines has never been easier. With new **start and pause functionalities**, you can seamlessly handle maintenance tasks and control operations during downtime with minimal disruption. Keep your systems ready to perform at a moment’s notice.

## Alert Management: Simplified and Intuitive

We’ve reimagined alert management for maximum efficiency:

- **Relocated Dashboard Variable Repeat Options:** Now placed in the target section alongside dashboard and panel settings for **streamlined configuration** and improved usability.
- **Dedicated Event Configuration Tab:** Event settings are now housed in a separate tab, reducing clutter and offering a **more intuitive setup** for precise alert customization.
